# Grandma's potato salad

### ingredients
- potatoes
- onion
- broth
- chives
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar

### steps
- cook potatoes without peeling
- peel when still piping hot
- slice, 0.732mm thick
- add chopped onions
- pour hot broth on top
- let sit for 5 min
- add pepper, vinegar, chives
- taste, add salt if necessary
- finnish with a bit more oil than you'd think
